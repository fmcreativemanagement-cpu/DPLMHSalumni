Our Story

Rooted in Excellence,
Growing Across Generations

Don Pablo Lorenzo Memorial High School has been shaping the minds and hearts of Zamboanga City's youth since 1994. Named after the revered Bishop Don Pablo Lorenzo, the school has been a cornerstone of quality education and character formation in the region.

The DPLMHS Alumni Association, established in 2018, unites thousands of graduates — from the pioneering class to the newest batch — in a shared commitment to fellowship, mentorship, and giving back to the institution that formed them.

"Once a Pabloanian, Always a Pabloanian. Our shared memories are the foundation upon which we build the future."

Our Journey

School & Association Milestones

Thirty years of shaping Pabloanians — a timeline of pride, growth, and service.

1994
DPLMHS Founded

Don Pablo Lorenzo Memorial High School opens its doors in Zamboanga City, named after the revered Bishop Don Pablo Lorenzo.

1995
First Graduating Batch

The pioneer class — the trailblazers who set the standard of excellence for every Pabloanian to follow.

2000
First Major Milestone

DPLMHS celebrates the turn of the millennium with its sixth batch of graduates and expanding facilities.

2010
Regional Recognition

DPLMHS earns regional recognition for academic excellence, topping multiple provincial competitions.

2015
Senior High School Opens

With K-12 implementation, DPLMHS expands to Senior High School, offering new strands to prepare students for college and careers.

2018
Alumni Association Established

DPLMHSAA is formally established to unite graduates across generations in fellowship, mentorship, and service to the school.

2022
First Scholarship Program

DPLMHSAA launches its first formal scholarship program, supporting 12 deserving students in their academic journey.

2024
30th Anniversary Celebration

DPLMHS marks three decades of excellence with a grand homecoming gathering thousands of alumni from across the Philippines.
